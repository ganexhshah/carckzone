Responsible Gaming

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Crackzone promotes safe and responsible participation in skill-based gaming contests.

1. Age Restriction

Paid participation is strictly limited to users aged 18 and above.

2. Play with Limits

Set personal limits for deposits, contest entry, and time spent in the app.

3. Play for Skill and Entertainment

Do not treat participation as guaranteed income. Join contests based on skill and budget control.

4. Warning Signs

Take a break if you feel stressed, are chasing losses, borrowing to play, or spending beyond your comfort limit.

5. Account Controls

Users may request temporary restrictions or account closure by contacting support.

6. Need Help

If gaming behavior affects your daily life, seek guidance from family, friends, or professional counseling services.
