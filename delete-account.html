Account Deletion Request

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

This page explains how users of Crackzone can request account deletion and what data is deleted or retained.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the Crackzone app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile/More and select Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the request.

If you cannot access the app, email deletion request to: hello.ganeshshah@gmail.com from your registered email/phone details.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Profile details (name, avatar, contact details).
  • Linked game IDs and non-essential preferences.
  • Push notification tokens and app session data.

What Data May Be Retained

  • Transaction and wallet records required for legal, tax, and anti-fraud compliance.
  • Security and audit logs required to prevent abuse and resolve disputes.

Retained compliance data is stored only for the legally required period, then deleted or anonymized.

Processing Time

We process verified deletion requests within 7 business days.
